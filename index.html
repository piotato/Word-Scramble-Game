Word Scramble Game

Gameplay

A simple word guessing game with the following gameplay:

  • The program will select any word and shuffle the position of the characters of that word.
  • The program will give you a hint and 10 seconds to think and answer.
  • Players will have 3 replays, each wrong guess, timeout, or refresh will deduct 1 turn.
  • When the player answers correctly, they will be awarded 1 point.
